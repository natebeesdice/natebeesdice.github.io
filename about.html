The monarchy

We have a small hive but they are very buzzy.

Toby: King Bee

Toby is the king of our little hive. He makes the sweet nector called dice and is the leading figure.

Nate: Busy Bee

Nate is the duke of our hive. He is the second in command and helps Toby make the dice and keep the hive running smoothly.

Blaze: Worker Bee

Blaze is a worker bee. He is responsible for selling the dice and keeping the hive clean and organized.
